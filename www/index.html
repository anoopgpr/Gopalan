About us

Gopalan Enterprises was founded in 1984 by Mr. C. Gopalan, an architect, with the objective of developing and constructing residential apartments in Bangalore. The company is one of the prominent real estate developers in Bangalore and has developed some of the finest residential apartments. It has the enviable record of having completed all its projects on time. The group is engaged in the promotion of Residential and Commercial properties along with Shopping Malls, Townships, Special Economic Zones, Software Technology Parks, Biotech Parks, Organic Farm, Educational Institutions, Cinemas, Hotels, Coworks, and Aerospace, etc.

We offer 30 years' experience in residential buildings in and around Bangalore for an astute clientele from around the world. We take pride in the superior craftsmanship and attention to detail is given to every residential and commercial projects which we construct. Our reputation nests on professional management, exceptional product quality, overall affordability and client service throughout the residential and commercial building process. Our commitment, integrity, teamwork and professional excellence has continually seen the growth and success of the real estate business since its inception in 1984.

Gopalan Enterprises bears the stamp of innovative design and superlative quality construction. The group's commitment to quality construction in flats, modernity in architectural value, space management, and customer satisfaction has set up a new trend among property developers in Bangalore and in India. Gopalan Enterprises has emerged as one of the competitors for the promotion of Special Economic Zone like Gopalan Global Axis, generating exports and employment. The group enjoys a giant presence in the Knowledge City and boasts of a prestigious global clientele.

Gopalan Enterprises brings you a combination of strong professional experience in real estate and a commitment to fresh, creative approaches for residential and commercial projects planning and design.

Our talents have been applied to residential and commercial projects throughout Bangalore. The Gopalan Group is committed to providing the highest standard of professional service. We are dedicated to the philosophy Inspired by Life, Built on Trust.

Our goal is to assist you by planning and designing facilities that meet your unique needs. We are supported by talented architects, interior designers, construction administration specialists, technical, marketing, customer support, and administrative personnel in our endeavor.
